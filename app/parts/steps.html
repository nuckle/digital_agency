Work Stages

Project Analysis

We familiarize ourselves with your business, study the niche specifics, analyze competitor activity, and define the target audience (TA).

Developing a Media Plan

We choose tools, create a media plan, and define objectives with you.

Campaign Creation

We set up Google Analytics, define goals in GA, and create target audiences. We draft ads, compile relevant commercial keywords, and define a list of negative keywords. Then, we launch the ads.

Optimization

After launching the ads, we analyze results and optimize the campaign objectives (automated optimization by algorithms).

Enhancement

We identify and implement additional tools and options within the system, fix any issues (if any), and adjust bids (cost-per-click, cost per conversion, etc.).

Report Preparation

We prepare and provide detailed reports on the work performed and the results of the launched Google Adwords campaigns, along with recommendations for future campaign management.
